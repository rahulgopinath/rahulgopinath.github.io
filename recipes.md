# Black Chickpeas Curry Katalakkari
കടലക്കറി

**Serves:** 4 | **Time:** 2 days in total

## Ingredients
- 1 cups katala
- 1 cup of grated coconut
- 4 shallots
- 1 tablespoon chillie power
- 1 tablespoon corriander
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 grampoo (clove)
- 2 pieces of garlic
- 1 cinnamon skin
- 1 tsp Fennel seeds (പെരുംജീരകം)


## Instructions
1. Sock chickpea in water for 6 hours
2. Roast the grated coconut
3. Put grated coconut + 1 Tsp chillie powder+1Tsp corriander+1tsp turmeric+1 clove+2 garlic+4 shallot+8 currileaves in a mixie and mix well.
4. Start the seasoning mix with 2 spoon coconut oil + 1 red chillie + mustard seeds + 8 curry leaves + season.
5. Add the mixed ingrediants + some water + bring to boil
